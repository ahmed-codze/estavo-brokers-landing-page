🔥 Frequently Asked Questions

Is Estavo Brokers a replacement for the broker?

Not at all. Estavo Brokers is a tool that helps the broker work better and faster, not a replacement. Experience and client relationships are your skills — Estavo just gives you the tools that make you stronger.

Does Estavo take a percentage of my commission?

No. Estavo is a subscription platform (SaaS), not a sales intermediary. You pay a fixed monthly subscription, and you take your commission 100% — Estavo doesn't take any part of it.

Is the data on the platform accurate?

Yes. All data on Estavo comes from reliable sources (developers, official websites) and we continuously update it. However, we always recommend you verify any information with the developer before giving a final promise to the client.

Is there training on using the platform?

The platform is very easy and doesn't need training. But if you need help, our team is available on WhatsApp and email to support you anytime.
