Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Estavo Brokers

Quick answers about the AI real estate platform built for Egyptian brokers.

What is Estavo Brokers?

Estavo Brokers is an AI-powered workspace built for real estate brokers in Egypt. It gives you a live picture of what's available in every project across New Cairo, Sheikh Zayed and the New Administrative Capital, with daily market updates on prices, payment plans, and units that have just been released or sold. You can search, compare availability, and generate professional PDF offers in seconds — so you answer your client faster than any other broker and close more deals.

Does Estavo offer a free trial?

Yes — Estavo Brokers offers a limited free trial so brokers can experience all core features, including the AI market advisor and PDF offer generator, before subscribing. The trial isn't a permanent free plan — after it ends, you choose the subscription plan that fits your work.

Does Estavo take a cut from my commission?

No — never. Estavo is a subscription platform (SaaS), not a sales middleman. You only pay a fixed monthly subscription, you close the deals yourself with your own clients, and you keep 100% of your commission — Estavo doesn't take a single pound from it. Our role is simply to give you the tools that make you faster and smarter. The commission is all yours.

Which Egyptian areas does Estavo cover?

Estavo covers all major Egyptian real estate markets — New Cairo, Sheikh Zayed, the New Administrative Capital, 6th of October, North Coast, and more — with verified data from developers and trusted sources, and continuous updates on availability and pricing.

How does the AI generate PDF offers?

You ask Estavo about a unit or compare projects. The AI prepares a structured, branded PDF offer with your name and your phone number — not Estavo's — along with the project details and payment plan, ready to send on WhatsApp in seconds.

Does Estavo support Arabic?

Yes — Estavo Brokers is fully bilingual (Arabic and English) and is built specifically for the Egyptian real estate market.
