Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  |  Applies to all Estavo Brokers users

1. Who We Are

Estavo Brokers ("Estavo" or "the Platform") is an AI-powered real estate search workspace designed for real estate brokers in Egypt. The platform provides access to a database of projects and units from Egyptian developers, an AI assistant to search and compare properties, and tools to generate professional PDF proposals.

The platform is operated by Estavo, based in Cairo, Egypt.

2. What We Collect

We only collect the following data:

  • Account information at registration: First name, phone number (your primary login identifier), work type (freelancer or brokerage company employee), and company name (optional).
  • AI assistant chat messages: Messages you send to the AI assistant are stored to maintain conversation context within your session.
  • Usage and analytics data: We use PostHog to track which features you use, which projects and developers you view, and your navigation patterns within the platform. This is used to improve user experience.
  • Error and performance data: We use Sentry to log technical errors and crash reports — this does not include the content of your personal messages.
  • Subscription data: Your plan type (free or paid) and subscription expiry date.

We do not collect payment card or banking details — subscriptions are managed via WhatsApp contact only.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Your clients' or prospects' personal data — PDFs are generated for your use only and we do not store your clients' information.
  • Geographic location or GPS data.
  • Any financial or investment data.

4. Why We Collect This Data

  • To operate the platform and provide the AI search assistant and property database features.
  • To maintain your account and manage your subscription.
  • To improve platform performance and fix bugs.
  • To send product updates or support messages (to registered users only).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to any third party. We share data only with the following infrastructure and service providers:

  • PostHog — usage analytics.
  • Sentry — error and performance tracking.
  • Cloud hosting providers — for platform operation.

All service providers operate under binding data processing agreements.

6. Messaging & Communication

Estavo only sends WhatsApp messages to users who have registered on the platform and provided their phone number. We may contact you regarding platform updates, subscription renewals, or technical support.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP or by contacting us at support@estavo.space. We honour opt-out requests within 24 hours.

7. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data — see our Data Deletion Instructions page for the full process.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Opt out of usage analytics by contacting support.

We respond to deletion requests within 30 business days.

8. Security

All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS. Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access controls. Estavo staff cannot access your personal chat messages except where strictly necessary for authorised technical support.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. For material changes, we will notify you via an in-platform message or WhatsApp before the change takes effect.

Questions about your privacy?

Get in touch and we'll respond as soon as possible.
