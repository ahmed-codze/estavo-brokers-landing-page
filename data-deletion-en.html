Privacy

Data Deletion Instructions

You can request permanent deletion of your personal data from Estavo Brokers at any time.

What Gets Deleted

When you submit a deletion request, we permanently delete the following:

  • Your account data: name, phone number, work type, company name.
  • Your AI assistant conversation history.
  • Your subscription data and plan type.
  • Usage data linked to your user ID in our analytics systems (PostHog).

After your request is processed you will no longer be able to access the platform with that account. You are free to create a new account at any time.

How to Request Deletion

⏱ We respond within 30 business days
  1. Send an email to support@estavo.space with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the phone number associated with your account so we can verify your identity.
  3. We will send a confirmation of receipt within 24 hours and complete the deletion within 30 business days.
  4. You will receive a final confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Quick Alternative: WhatsApp

You can also send a deletion request via WhatsApp to +20 106 952 8393 with the message: "Data Deletion Request" and your registered phone number.
